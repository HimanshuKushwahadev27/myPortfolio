Astracore - Distributed Rate Limiter

A production-grade microservices platform with centralized Redis-backed rate limiting, idempotency enforcement, and atomic stock management.

  • Built Redis-backed sliding-window rate limiting, idempotency enforcement, and atomic stock updates with Lua scripts to handle concurrent requests without distributed locks.
  • Designed a reusable Maven module consumed across services, encapsulating rate limiting, idempotency, cache abstraction, and namespaced Redis key management to eliminate duplication.
  • Integrated Keycloak with OAuth2/OIDC and PKCE for the Angular SPA, while Flyway managed versioned schema migrations for zero-drift deployments.
  • Dockerized the stack across infra and service layers, with Redis, PostgreSQL, and Keycloak as stateful dependencies and Spring Boot services auto-migrating schemas on startup.
